Features

Job Junction is an Indian-based global employment website owned and operated by 2Breed Worldwide, Inc. It was created in 1999 through the merger of 2Breed and Online Career Center (OCC)., Job Junction is one of the most trafficked employment websites in India. The company has approximately 5,000 employees on its payroll in 36 countries

In 2008, Job Junction had over a million job postings at any time and over 1 million resumes, in the database and over 63 million job seekers per month.
